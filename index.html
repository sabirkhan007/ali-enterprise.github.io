About Us

Welcome to Ali Enterprises

Ali Enterprises, established in 2010 by founder Imtiyaz Ali in New Delhi, has become a prominent name in the jeans production and sales industry in North India. The company's commitment to quality, style, and customer satisfaction has solidified its position as one of the premier jeans sellers in the region.

Ali Enterprises offers two distinctive brands: "Flow Bull" and "Top Cross," each catering to a diverse range of customer preferences. "Flow Bull" embodies a fusion of contemporary design and comfort, making it a favorite among those seeking trendy and stylish denim options. On the other hand, "Top Cross" is known for its classic and timeless appeal, appealing to customers who prefer a more traditional denim experience.
